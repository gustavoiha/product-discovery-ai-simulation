# Product Roadmap Recommendation

## Product Summary
Mobile-first product for SMB owners managing operations on the go.

## Prioritization Method
Weighted score = (reach x impact x confidence x strategic_fit) / effort - niche_penalty

## Accepted Signals
- Mobile completion speed is critical.
- Simpler navigation lowers support demand.

## Rejected / Niche Signals
- Rare request for desktop-only complex scripting mode.

- Score: 26.0

## Next

- Score: 20.4

## Later

- Score: 11.7

## Deprioritized Feedback
- Niche power-user customization for less than 2% of users.

## Hypotheses
- Faster mobile completion improves weekly retention.
- Reducing navigation steps lowers abandonment.
